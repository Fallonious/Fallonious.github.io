The who, what, why

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

After being in marketing and operations roles at start-ups for nearly a decade,
I wanted to bring my talents to South Beach gain experience on the technical side. I'm insatiably curious and I enjoy learning about new things.


With an apptitute for organization, I enjoy implementing the structure that enables business to grow. A firm believer that there is always room for refinement, I've applied this belief to my ongoing education.
